Features to replace all your other tools

Interactive Map and Virtual Tours

  • 360-degree Virtual Tours: Provide immersive 360-degree views of popular tourist destinations, hotels, landmarks, and more.
  • Interactive City Maps: Allow users to explore destinations with interactive maps that highlight points of interest, restaurants, hotels, and transport options.

Local Experiences & Activities

  • Bookings for Local Experiences: Integrate booking options for guided tours, cooking classes, adventure activities, local workshops, etc.
  • Live Events and Festivals: Highlight upcoming local events, festivals, and exhibitions in each city or region.

Real-Time Travel Assistance

  • Live Chat with Virtual Guides: Provide real-time assistance through live chat with a virtual tour guide or chatbot to answer questions about the destination.
  • Emergency Information: Include emergency contact numbers (e.g., local police, embassy) and medical facilities.

Personalized Recommendations

  • AI-Powered Recommendations: Use AI to suggest destinations, activities, and experiences based on user preferences (e.g., budget, travel style, previous trips).
  • In-depth Destination Guides: Provide detailed information on countries, cities, or regions, including history, food, attractions, and local tips.

Multi-language Support

  • Language Options: Voice assitant and live translator along with 48+ languages.
  • Cultural Adaptations: Tailor recommendations and travel advice based on the user's country of origin.
